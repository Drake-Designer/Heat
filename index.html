Turn Up the Groove!

We are all about feel-good dance and some good funk! We mix old-school grooves with a fresh sound that makes people move and have fun.

Our songs are full of energy, rhythm, and good vibes. Whether we're on stage or in the studio, we just love making music that brings people together and gets them dancing.

The Band

Heat is a dance/funk band based in Dublin. We’re a group of friends with a big love for music and live energy. Our sound is inspired by artists like Daft Punk, Chromeo, and especially Franc Moody. The band started in 2019 with Shaneo (vocals), Ati (bass), and Jorge (keys). JT joined on guitar in 2020, and Dario completed the lineup on drums in 2023. Since then, we’ve been crafting a unique style that mixes funk grooves, analog synths, and punchy rhythms to get people moving. We’ve spent years rehearsing at Yellow Door Music Studio, also recording at home. We’ve also played live in great venues around Dublin like Bello Bar and The Sugar Club, always bringing energy and good vibes to the stage.
