Dance & Funk vibes from Dublin

Heat is a Dublin-based funk/dance band blending vintage grooves with modern energy. Influenced by the likes of Daft Punk, Chromeo, and Franc Moody, our sound is crafted to keep you moving from the first beat to the last chorus.

Formed in 2023 by a group of friends with a shared love for rhythm and performance, Heat brings together slick basslines, analog synths, and punchy drums for a high-energy live experience.
